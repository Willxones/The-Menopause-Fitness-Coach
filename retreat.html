Fitness & Hiking Luxury Retreat

Riversdale Lodge, Bourne End, Buckinghamshire · Four uplifting days curated by Alison Jones & Rachel Edwards

Reserve with a £100 deposit Discover more

Crafted With Care by Ally & Rachel

It is with great excitement that Ally Jones and Rachel Edwards warmly invite you to join us for a truly special experience at the beautiful Riversdale Lodge in the charming village of Bourne End.

Over four days and three nights you will move, breathe, learn and restore. Enjoy invigorating 6–8+ mile guided walks, three energising morning fitness classes, fascia breathing, meditation, navigation workshops, and nourishing meals cooked with care. Wild swimming is on offer if conditions allow, and the large outdoor plunge pool is always ready for a refreshing dip.

Whether you are new to strength training or looking to deepen your connection with nature, this retreat is designed to leave you uplifted, empowered, and energised.

Retreat Snapshot
  • Guided adventures

    Two countryside hikes (6–8+ miles · 3–4 hours) through riverside paths and rolling hills.

  • Expert coaching

    Strength, navigation, breathwork and meditation sessions led by Alison Jones (BWLA PT) & Rachel Edwards (Lowland Leader).

  • Restorative moments

    Wholesome seasonal food, riverside relaxation, plunge pool dips and quiet time to reflect.
